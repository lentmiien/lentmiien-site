# Generated image assets

All ten images were generated specifically for this experience with the built-in image-generation tool. The shared direction was **photorealistic cinematic paleoart / natural-history reconstruction**, with believable atmosphere, a clear focal subject, center-safe mobile crops, and room for either a left- or right-aligned glass panel.

The generated PNG originals are 1672×941. Project finals were normalized with local FFmpeg to 1600×900 WebP at quality 86. No third-party image URL is used at runtime.

Every prehistoric scene is an artist’s reconstruction, not a photograph. Animal colors, some soft tissues, and most depicted moment-to-moment behavior are uncertain.

## Final generation prompts

### `deep-time-gateway.webp`

> A 16:9 opening for a children’s dinosaur-history journey: a dark natural-history observatory carved into a fossil-rich sandstone canyon, opening toward an immense fern-and-conifer prehistoric landscape at amber dawn. One small, plausible early dinosaur silhouette is distant and centered for scale. Photorealistic cinematic paleoart, graphite shadow, ember stone, golden light, broad safe zones on both sides and a meaningful center crop. No mixed-era crowd, people, labels, pseudo-text, logos, signatures, or watermark.

### `triassic-beginnings.webp`

> Late Triassic Pangaea around 220 million years ago: two slender Coelophysis-like early theropods beside a shallow pool on a hot red-mud floodplain, with conifers, cycads, horsetails, mesas, and one larger crocodile-line archosaur far away. Low wide camera, true small scale, open right-side atmosphere, center-safe action, cinematic copper light. No grass, flowers, modern birds, giant monster proportions, pronated hands, text, or watermark.

### `jurassic-giants.webp`

> Morrison Formation floodplain around 150 million years ago after rain: an anatomically plausible Diplodocus crosses shallow water while a Stegosaurus family browses nearby; any Allosaurus is only a distant silhouette. Conifers, tree ferns, horsetails, clearing storm, photorealistic natural-history reconstruction, calm grandeur, center-safe subjects and darker left-side panel space. No tail dragging, rearing, staged combat, grass, text, or watermark.

### `feathers-before-flight.webp`

> A Late Jurassic Solnhofen island lagoon: one Archaeopteryx-like feathered theropod launches from a conifer branch, showing long feathered wings, long bony tail, wing claws, and a subtle toothy jaw; a second small individual perches behind. Detailed feather barbs, blue-green water, graphite bark, amber rim light, full uncut silhouette, calm right-side space. No modern beak, oversized eagle form, pterosaurs, modern flowers, text, or watermark.

### `cretaceous-spinosaurus.webp`

> North Africa about 96 million years ago: an anatomically plausible Spinosaurus wades belly-deep at a tidal river margin, with narrow snout, tall thin sail, and paddle-like tail visible. Humid channels, muddy sandbars, palms, early flowering plants, distant fish, storm-lit teal and amber atmosphere. Show it near shore rather than asserting a high-speed swimmer. No Tyrannosaurus, Velociraptor, four-legged monster pose, mixed-time crowd, text, or watermark.

### `gobi-velociraptor.webp`

> Mongolia around 72 million years ago: a correctly small, feather-covered Velociraptor steps near a seasonal stream while two Protoceratops guard a partly buried nest in the middle distance. Wind-carved orange dunes, sparse plants, distant blue mountains, turkey-like scale, wing-like feathered arms, stiff tail, warm dust light, center-safe composition. No oversized movie raptor, pack attack, pronated wrists, text, or watermark.

### `last-neighbors.webp`

> Hell Creek about 67 million years ago: a mostly scaled adult Tyrannosaurus rex pauses on one riverbank while an adult and juvenile Triceratops browse on the other. Humid dawn mist, cypress-like woodland, ferns, flowering shrubs, one tiny bird in the sky, wide ecosystem portrait with safe outer edges. Calm awareness, no attack, gore, roaring, exposed permanent grin, fully feathered adult T. rex, Stegosaurus, Velociraptor, text, or watermark.

### `fossil-detectives.webp`

> A modern badlands paleontology dig in documentary-photography style: a diverse adult field team in practical sun protection carefully brushes a partly exposed theropod skeleton, measures a three-toed trackway, takes photographs, and records notes. Plausible grid, plaster jackets and hand tools, amber morning light, evidence central-left and open rock at right. No treasure-hunter stereotypes, readable writing, logos, bare-handed fossil handling, or watermark.

### `chicxulub-impact.webp`

> A distant latest-Cretaceous coastal wetland view of the Chicxulub impact beginning: brilliant horizon flash, expanding ejecta plume, shock-lit graphite clouds, dark water and forest foreground. Small non-bird dinosaur silhouettes move along the far shore while a tiny bird-like silhouette shelters in branches. Panoramic, non-graphic, scientifically weighty rather than fantasy. No city, modern animals, instant-death tableau, lava fantasy, text, or watermark.

### `birds-continue.webp`

A raven-sized modern bird launches from a central-left ledge with full wings, individual feathers, scaly feet and curved claws visible; distant birds cross the amber valley. Photorealistic wildlife cinema with safe edges and center crop. No fantasy dinosaur shadow, morphing effect, non-bird dinosaur, human, signage, text, or watermark.
