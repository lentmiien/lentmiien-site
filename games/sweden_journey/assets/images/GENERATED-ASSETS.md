# Generated Sweden journey artwork

These eight project assets were created with the built-in image-generation tool, then downscaled to 1600×900 WebP at quality 86. All prompts requested full-screen, text-free, logo-free, watermark-free 16:9 artwork. The source PNGs remain in the Codex generated-image store; the WebP files here are the application deliverables.

## `archipelago-hero.webp`

Use case: photorealistic-natural. A breathtaking Swedish summer evening from a quiet granite island in the Stockholm archipelago, looking toward distant red timber cottages and a small sailboat beneath a luminous Nordic sky. Clear Baltic water, Scots pine silhouettes, scattered low islands, and a soft midnight-sun glow. Cinematic editorial landscape photography with authentic textures, Swedish blue and natural golden light, restrained graphite shadows, and edge breathing room for interface overlays. No people in the foreground, flag, fantasy aurora, text, logos, watermark, tropical vegetation, or travel-poster oversaturation.

## `viking-routes.webp`

Use case: historical-scene. Viking Age Sweden around the tenth century: a Swedish trading longship leaving a sheltered Baltic inlet toward eastern trade routes. A weathered runestone, simple landing, furs and trade goods, practical period clothing, clinker-built ship, and striped wool sail. Archaeological documentary realism at misty amber dawn, emphasizing trade, travel, and connection. No horned helmets, battle, fantasy armor, modern objects, readable runes, text, logos, or watermark.

## `vasa-1628.webp`

Use case: historical-scene. Stockholm harbor in summer 1628, moments before the richly carved Vasa begins its maiden voyage. A towering early-seventeenth-century wooden warship, period quays, buildings, workboats, clothing, sailcloth, rigging, and cold Baltic water. Museum-grade reconstruction with proud northern morning light and a subtle sense of fragility. No sinking spectacle, pirate styling, modern skyline, readable banners, text, logos, or watermark.

## `democracy-1921.webp`

Use case: historical-scene. A Swedish parliamentary election day in 1921, the first election under universal and equal suffrage, as an ordinary but momentous civic morning. Women and men in accurate everyday clothing calmly queue outside a modest Stockholm polling place with cobblestones and bicycles. Cinematic colorized documentary realism, cool morning and low amber sun. No propaganda, readable signs, modern objects, rally spectacle, text, logos, or watermark.

No people, costume shorthand, simultaneous aurora, Alpine peaks, tourism clichés, text, logos, or watermark.
