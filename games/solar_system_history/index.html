    Science notes

    What you are seeing

    This is a curated story, not a numerical orbital model. Planet sizes, distances, orbital speeds, camera paths, and the spacing between events are deliberately compressed. Generated artwork is labeled as an artist’s impression.

    How uncertainty is shown

    Cards distinguish strong evidence from indirect evidence, leading hypotheses, and model-based projections. The Moon-forming impact is a family of leading models. A sharp late bombardment spike is disputed. Earth’s physical survival during the Sun’s giant phases remains unresolved.
